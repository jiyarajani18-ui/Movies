🎞️ My Top Picks

Inception (2010)

Directed by Christopher Nolan, this sci-fi thriller dives into dreams within dreams. The stunning visuals and complex storyline make it unforgettable.

Interstellar (2014)

An emotional and scientific journey across galaxies, exploring love, time, and humanity’s survival. A masterpiece that connects science and emotion.

The Dark Knight (2008)

This superhero classic redefined the genre with its dark tone, brilliant storytelling, and Heath Ledger’s legendary performance as the Joker.

Forrest Gump (1994)

A heartwarming journey through history and life lessons, showing how kindness, love, and perseverance make all the difference.

Titanic (1997)

James Cameron’s iconic romantic drama that sails through love, loss, and destiny on the ill-fated ship. A timeless emotional classic.
